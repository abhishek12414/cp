Project overview

this project is designed for the vehicle service provider and the user who require their vehicle service, upgrading parts and get the toe service if their vehicle get damaged while traveling or at home.

they can open the app and find the nearest service point with in some radius of 5km to 10km and book a service.

also they can search the service provider who have option to provide parts/service.

as a service provider we only require their business and physical address location along with the service they are offering.

and as a consumer/user, i can look about a service or offering near to me.

i might search a provider who can be outside my location as well
